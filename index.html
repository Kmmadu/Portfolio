Complete Office Network Outage Resolved Through VLAN Reconfiguration

Problem:

A client reported a complete internet outage across their office network. Initial checks confirmed physical connectivity, but users were unable to access online services.

Investigation:

Traced the PoE cable path from the router to the wireless radio, performed direct laptop connection tests to isolate the failure point, and logged into the MikroTik router using WinBox. Configuration audit revealed that all VLAN assignments (including VLAN 1 and 2) had been cleared from the main bridge interface after a recent power surge, breaking network segmentation and DHCP relay to clients.

Resolution:

Reconfigured the required VLAN settings through Winbox, restored proper network segmentation, and verified successful internet connectivity across the client environment.
